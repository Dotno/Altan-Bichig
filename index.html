Altan-Bichig

A critical edition of the Cult of Chinggis Khan transmitted texts, edited by Dotno D. Pount as a part of a PhD dissertation (pending completion and defense), under the supervision of Prof. Chris Atwood.

This is a hypertext created using TEI. Lots of codes and inspirations were taken from the XSL transformation at TEI-CAT, written by Marjorie Burghart.
